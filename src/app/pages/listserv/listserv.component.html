To subscribe to this list: Visit https://lists.uw.edu/postorius/lists/ipcc.lists.uw.edu/ - or - Email to ipcc-join@lists.uw.edu with 'subscribe' in the subject line, and follow further instructions sent to your email.
To post to this list: Email ipcc@lists.uw.edu from your subscribed email address.
To learn how to unsubscribe or make adjustments to your membership: Email ipcc-request@lists.uw.edu with 'help' in the subject line. You will receive an email response with further instructions.
